When They Say "You Can Speak To Me"
```
1
Don't pitch. Give just enough to intrigue:
"Of course. So basically I already built a website for [Business Name] — it's ready, I just need the right person to see it. Whoever makes the decision — if they like it, it's available. If not, no problem. Can you let your boss know? I'll have it ready to send by tomorrow morning."
2
Then immediately go for the number:
"Please what's the best number to reach him directly so I can send it myself?"
If they give the number →
3
"Perfect. I'll send it to him directly tomorrow morning. What's his name please?"
If they won't give the number →
3
"No problem. Please just let him know — tomorrow morning I'll call back and ask for him directly. What time is he usually in?"
```

1
"That's fair — can I ask, is it bringing you consistent bookings? Because most shortlet websites in Lagos are just sitting there doing nothing. I'm not here to replace it, I just want to show you what one that actually converts looks like. If you like it, it's yours. If not, we don't have to speak again."
"My Boss is a Developer / Stopped Building"
1
"Are you trying to say you guys don't want extra bookings from a website?"
2
If they soften — close immediately:
"I've already built it. I just want to show it — if he likes it, it's available for ₦150k. If not, we don't have to speak again. Can I send the link?"
